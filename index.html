How it works

Four steps.
Fully automatic.

Vault Fee is a fee-powered reward system built on Solana. Every time someone buys or sells the VaultFee token, a percentage of that transaction is automatically routed into a shared on-chain vault.

That vault accumulates real SOL — not synthetic tokens, not points. At regular distribution intervals, the vault pays out proportionally to every holder based on how much they hold and how long they have been holding.

The hold multiplier is the key differentiator: the longer you hold, the higher your reward share. Selling resets your multiplier — committed holders are structurally rewarded over short-term traders.

VaultFee — Documentation

Documentation

Everything you need to understand how Vault Fee works — from the vault mechanism to reward distribution and the hold multiplier system.

Overview

What is Vault Fee?

Vault Fee is a Solana-based token that converts trading activity into real, ongoing SOL rewards for holders. Every transaction contributes to a shared vault. Holders receive SOL from this vault, creating a direct link between market activity and holder earnings.

Core Mechanism

How the Vault Works

Every buy and sell contributes a percentage directly to the on-chain vault treasury. The vault grows with trading volume, and at regular intervals SOL is distributed to all holders.

  • Every buy and sell contributes a fee to the vault
  • Vault grows continuously with trading volume
  • Holders receive SOL rewards at each cycle
  • Longer hold time increases rewards
Reward Structure

Distribution Cycles

Rewards are distributed in recurring cycles. Distribution scales with both wallet size and holding duration, ensuring alignment between long-term holders and overall ecosystem growth.

  • Distributions occur every 30 minutes
  • All distributions are verifiable on-chain
  • Larger positions receive proportionally larger rewards
  • Longer hold time increases your multiplier
Hold Multiplier

Why Holding Matters

Long-term holders receive increased rewards while selling resets participation advantages. The longer and larger your position, the more you benefit from the vault.

  • Long-term holders receive an increased reward share
  • Selling resets your hold multiplier back to baseline
  • Larger and longer positions benefit the most
  • Natural incentive to hold over short-term trading
Getting Started

How to Participate

Simply hold the VAULT token in any Solana wallet. Rewards are distributed automatically — no staking or manual steps required.

  • Purchase VaultFee on Pump.fun using any Solana wallet
  • Hold tokens to accumulate your hold-time multiplier
  • SOL rewards are distributed automatically to your wallet
  • Check the dashboard to track your vault earnings
